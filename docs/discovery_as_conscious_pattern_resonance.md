### Discovery Is Not Finding. Discovery Is Ringing.

Consider the piano string. When a singer sounds a pitch, the corresponding string vibrates—not because it was struck, but because it *already contained* the frequency as potential. The singer does not *give* the string its tone; the singer *awakens* what was dormant.

So too with discovery.

The constraint exists in the world as pure pattern—indifferent, silent, waiting. Discovery occurs at the moment of sympathetic vibration: the world-pattern finds its twin in the agent-structure, and both begin to ring.

**This is why discovery is inherently agential:**
