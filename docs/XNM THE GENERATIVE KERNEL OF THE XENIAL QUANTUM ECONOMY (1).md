  - Growth (is the number getting bigger?)

  much but not whether it matters.

  direction:

  τₖ ranges from 0 (perfect incoherence, value destruction) to 1 (perfect
  coherence, generative integration).

  resonance analysis:

  3. Niche Uniqueness - Does this work occupy a distinct position in
    value-space, or does it compete for saturated bandwidth?
  4. Recursive Encoding - Does this work improve the agent's capacity for
    future coherence, or extract present value at future cost?
  5. Threshold Beauty - Does this work exhibit self-similar coherence at
    multiple scales, or is it locally optimized but globally incoherent?

  Critically: τₖ can only be measured retrospectively.

  You cannot know in advance whether your work will prove coherent. You can

  - Do later participants look back and see your contribution as
